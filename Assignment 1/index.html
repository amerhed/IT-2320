Brand New

Genre: Rock/Emo

Members:

  • Jesse Lacey
  • Vin Accardi
  • Brian Lane
  • Garrett Tierney

Brand New is a Rock/Emo band from Long Island, New York that formed in 2000.
They have five studio albums with the most recent came out in August of 2017.

Your Favorite Weapon 2001
Deja Entendu 2003
The Devil and God Are Raging Inside Me 2006
Daisy 2009
Science Fiction 2017
